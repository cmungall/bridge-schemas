    Class: EggnogMapperAnnotations

    EggNOG-mapper v2 functional annotations for genes. Provides: - COG functional categories - GO term annotations - KEGG pathway/ortholog mappings - EC enzyme numbers - PFAM domain annotations

    COVERAGE: 93,558,330 annotated genes (~9% of total)

    COG CATEGORY DISTRIBUTION (most common): - S (function unknown): Most common - J (translation): ~8% - K (transcription): ~6% - C (energy): ~5% - E (amino acid metabolism): ~5%

    URI: https://w3id.org/kbase/kbase_ke_pangenome/EggnogMapperAnnotations
