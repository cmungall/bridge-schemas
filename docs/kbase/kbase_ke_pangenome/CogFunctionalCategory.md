# Enum: CogFunctionalCategory 




_COG (Clusters of Orthologous Groups) single-letter functional categories. Multi-letter combinations indicate proteins with multiple functions._

_CATEGORY FREQUENCIES IN DATABASE: - S (unknown): Most common - J (translation): ~8% of annotations - K (transcription): ~6% - C (energy): ~5% - E (amino acids): ~5%_
